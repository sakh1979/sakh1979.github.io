Reminiscence of the Olden Days

Kyle Train Depot and Heritage Center

Posted on May 31, 2017

Kyle Train Depot and Heritage Center offers a wealth of historical knowledge, and should be a very tempting venue for history enthusiasts.

I was however, attracted by the rustic looking buildings and backgrounds that were obviously photogenic. Below are some pictures of Ari, my family friend’s daughter, I took while visiting the place.

Picture 3

Highlight on her right is from ambient light via an open door.

Picture 4

The prop, in this picture a luggage, was laying around the heritage center.

Picture 5

Notice the old school telephone, and typewriter?

Camera Settings

  • Shutter Speed: 1/120th
