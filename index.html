Nifty Fifty During a Sunset

Posted on April 10, 2016

I love shooting during sunset, it gives picture a magical look. But, shooting in the dusk, especially with the sun in the background, comes with its own challenges. There are few ways to shoot around the constraints: Correctly exposing the background, this will under expose the subject. Sometimes turning the subject into a silhouette. If that’s the look you are going for, then this method is perfect. Correctly exposing the subject, by far the most common method used by natural light photographers. [Read More]
